Illuviation
Enrichment of a horizon with materials from another horizon transported by the flow of water in the soil.
These materials can be, silicate clays, organic matter, iron, calcium carbonates among others.
Illuviation involves translocation and reactions that immobilize the translocated constituent.

Illuvial horizon
An illuvial horizon is one in which the materials transported by the illuviation have accumulated.
