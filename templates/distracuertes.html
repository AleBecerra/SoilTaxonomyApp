Very strongly acid pH (and low salinity) in the upper 50 cm.
The pH is < 4.5 in 0.01 M CaCl2 (5.0 or less in 1:1 water). Electrical conductivity in the saturation extract is < 4.0 dS/m. The pH and salinity criteria must be met in at least 25 cm of the upper 50 cm.
