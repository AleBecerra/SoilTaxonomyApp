Cryoturbation is the mixing of the soil as a result of intense frost churning due to freeze-thaw cycles.
Evidence of cryoturbation includes:
        a. Irregular or broken horizons.
        b. Involutions.
        c. Organic matter accumulation on the permafrost table.
        d. Orientated rock fragments.
        e. Silt caps on rock fragments.
