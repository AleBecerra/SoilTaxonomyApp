A thick, dark-colored, humus-rich horizon in which organic carbon is associated with poorly crystalline, short-range-order minerals or aluminumhumus complexes. A color value of 2.5 or less, moist, and chroma of 2 or less. Horizon has a color value of 2.5 or less, moist, and chroma of 2 or less and an overall average of organic carbon > 6%, by weight.
