Soil temperature regime

Each pedon has a characteristic temperature regime that can be measured and described.
For most practical purposes, the temperature regime can be described by the average annual soil temperature and the average seasonal fluctuations within the main root zone (depth from 5 to 100 cm)

Nine temperature regimes are recognized:
• Cryic
• Frigid
• Hyperthermic
• Isofrigid
• Isohyperthermic
• Isomesic
• Isothermic
• Mesic
• Thermic
