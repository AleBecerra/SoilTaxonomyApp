The control section is the vertical section that is located at a certain distance below the surface or mineral soil or the upper limit of a specific horizon.
For Entisols: between the lower boundary of an Ap horizon or a depth of 25 cm below the mineral soil surface, whichever is deeper, and the shallower of the following:
(a) a depth of 100 cm below the mineral soil surface, or
(b) a root-limiting layer.
